The Power of Propolis

Propolis, often called "bee glue," is one of nature's most powerful healing compounds. Bees create this resinous substance to protect their hives from bacteria, viruses, and fungi.

Our research has identified propolis as containing over 300 beneficial compounds, including:

  • Flavonoids: Powerful antioxidants that protect against free radical damage
  • Phenolic acids: Anti-inflammatory compounds that reduce skin irritation
  • Terpenes: Antimicrobial agents that prevent infection and promote healing
  • Amino acids: Building blocks for healthy skin regeneration
