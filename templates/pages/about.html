About Ishtar Naturals

Pioneering the future of natural skincare through the perfect harmony of ancient wisdom and modern science.

Our Story

Ishtar Naturals was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to harness the incredible healing properties of bee products and enhance them with cutting-edge nanotechnology.

Our journey began when our founder, Dr. Bahaa Ilyas, a PhD in Theoretical Physics, discovered the untapped potential of propolis - nature's most powerful healing compound. Through extensive research and development, we've created a revolutionary extraction method that maximizes the therapeutic benefits of bee-derived ingredients.

Today, we're proud to offer premium skincare products that address real problems faced by millions of people worldwide. Our commitment to natural ingredients, scientific innovation, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.

Our Mission

To provide natural, effective skincare solutions that heal, protect, and nourish through the power of bee-derived ingredients enhanced by advanced science.

Our Vision

To become the world's leading provider of scientifically-enhanced natural skincare products, setting new standards for quality and effectiveness.

Our Values

Natural purity, scientific excellence, customer satisfaction, and sustainable practices guide every decision we make and every product we create.
