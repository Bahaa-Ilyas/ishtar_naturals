Our Clinical Studies

At Ishtar Naturals, we are committed to scientific rigor and evidence-based product development. Our formulations undergo thorough testing and, where applicable, are supported by independent clinical studies to validate their efficacy and safety.

We collaborate with leading dermatologists and research institutions to conduct studies that assess the performance of our key ingredients and finished products. These studies help us understand the benefits of our unique blend of bee products and nanotechnology on various skin concerns, ensuring that our claims are backed by scientific data.

While specific study details are often proprietary, we are transparent about our commitment to research. Our focus areas include:

  • **Hydration and Barrier Function**: Studies on how our products improve skin moisture retention and strengthen the skin's natural protective barrier.
  • **Anti-inflammatory Effects**: Research into the soothing and calming properties of propolis and other bee-derived ingredients on irritated skin.
  • **Antimicrobial Activity**: Investigations into the natural antiseptic qualities of our formulations, particularly for products like deodorants.
  • **Nanotechnology Efficacy**: Studies demonstrating the enhanced delivery and absorption of active ingredients due to our advanced nanotechnology.

We continuously strive to expand our scientific understanding and will update this section with more detailed findings as they become publicly available. Our goal is to provide you with products that are not only luxurious but also deliver measurable and visible results.
